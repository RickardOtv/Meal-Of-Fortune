Meal of Fortune

Spin the Wheel to Find Where to Eat

How It Works

Can't decide where to eat? Meal of Fortune takes the stress out of choosing. Navigate the map to your area, search for nearby restaurants, and spin the fortune wheel — it randomly picks a restaurant for you.

Features

  • Interactive fortune wheel that randomly selects a nearby restaurant
  • Google Maps integration with real-time restaurant data
  • Filter by cuisine type, price range, rating, and open status
  • Search restaurants and cafes in any location worldwide
  • Fun confetti celebration when the wheel picks your meal

Free Restaurant Picker

Meal of Fortune is a free web app that helps groups and individuals decide where to eat. Whether you're looking for nearby restaurants, a specific cuisine, or just want to try something new — spin the wheel and let fortune decide.
